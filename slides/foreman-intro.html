Smart-proxies

A Smart proxy helps Foreman orchestrate the process of commissioning a new host. It resides on the network segment of the server that performs the specific function, and can control DNS, DHCP, TFTP, Puppet CA, and BMC.

Config Management

Reports, facts, classes and environment are imported from the Puppet Master / Chef Server. Foreman serves as a node classifier and parameter store for the puppet master. Foreman can manage multiple Puppet masters and Puppet environments.

Compute resources

Foreman manages bare metal systems as well as RHEV-M, oVirt, EC2, VMware, OpenStack, RackSpace, Google Compute Engine and libvirt.
