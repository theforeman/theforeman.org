GET /api/puppetclasses/:id
顯示 Puppet 類別

GET /api/hosts/:host_id/puppetclasses/:id
顯示主機的 Puppet 類別

GET /api/hostgroups/:hostgroup_id/puppetclasses/:id

GET /api/environments/:environment_id/puppetclasses/:id
顯示環境的 Puppet 類別
