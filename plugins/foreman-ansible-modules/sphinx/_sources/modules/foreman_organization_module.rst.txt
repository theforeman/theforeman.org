Examples
--------

.. code-block:: yaml+jinja

    
    - name: "Create CI Organization"
      foreman_organization:
        username: "admin"
        password: "changeme"
        server_url: "https://foreman.example.com"
        name: "My Cool New Organization"
        state: present
